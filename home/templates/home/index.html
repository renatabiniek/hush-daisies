Our Studio

We are Alice and Lily, wife & wife, and we create dry flower bouquets, wreaths and home decorations from our cozy studio in the middle of north county Dublin.

The idea for Hush Daises was born 2 years ago, when we moved to Ireland from the hectic East London. Inspired by the abundance of natural beauty surrounding us, we started to (re-)discover the joy of creating, and soon we were spellbound by the art of drying and preserving flowers.

We love how versitile dry flowers can be, and we create unique designs for home, special occassions, different seasons and even weddings! We hand-craft our arrangements with flowers and reeds that are sourced locally, with lots of love and attention to detail.

Have a look at our ready-made bunches in our shop or get in touch with us with a special request. We'd love to make something unique just for you.

If you find yourself in the area, we regularly organise themed workshops in Malahide, so join us to learn a new skill or just to have some craic!
